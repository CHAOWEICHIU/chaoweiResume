What should Rethink Robotics do in China?

Partnership
It is very important to partner with at least one local firm in order to accomplish many tasks:

(1)
Understand manufacturing environment in China and deal with surprises easily by using the help of local firm.

(2)
Have up-to-date regulation information in order to have more time to focus on customer service instead of continuously dealing with complex regulations.

(3)
Accommodate the fact that China is at the stage that it has enough capacity to build its own empire with its accumulated assets (people, money and skills). It leads to a situation where more than 75% of foreign businesses are less welcome than before in China.
