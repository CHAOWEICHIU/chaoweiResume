Industrial Robot In China?

There are plenty of robotic companies that have come and gone in the past 20 years. It is a little bit late for a new company to think about getting into this industry; however, a company can still have a share with proper marketing and business strategy based on up-to-date information.





Need Industrial Robot?

Factory Booming
Manufacturing plants have increased dramatically over the past 10 years because the government encourages the development of domestic plants with low-cost loans and factory property incentives. As a result, there are countless plants in China.

Why … Industrial Robot?
The cost for producing each product has been increased dramatically for the past five years due to three critical reasons:

Age
Aging and diminishing factory-age workforce by the one-child policy of past regimes.

Cost
High cost of training and housing for a short-term transient workforce.

Wage
Average wage increased rapidly over the past five years.

What Marketing Strategy?

Three Types of Clients
There are cost, comprehensive service, and specialty types of clients in China. Each company tends to target its own type of clients based on its own capacity, ability and resources in the robotics industry.



What Business Strategy?

Localization
Localization is used because there are many national policies implemented that might significantly affect a foreign enterprise’s business without advanced warning. Also, cultural differences would affect how the first line of workers operate a company’s robot. Therefore, I think it is very important to hire a person who has plant-manufacturing experience in China and is able to speak English and Mandarin. If the company lacks an employee with these skills, it should partner with another local robotics company.
