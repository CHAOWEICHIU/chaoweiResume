What Should Rethink Robotics do in China?

There are plenty of robotic companies that have come and gone in the past 20 years. It is a little bit late if a company just thought about getting into this industry. However, a company still can have a share with proper marketing and business strategy based on up-to-date information.




Why industrial Robot?

Manufacturing Plant Booming
Government mandates and political encouragement to develop the industry domestically and ultimately to export product with low-cost loans and factory property incentives. Also, serious investment in automation and robotics from companies such as Foxconn and Apple that manufacture their goods in China.

But ... why not people?
There are three segments in this discussion. Firstly, wage increases rapidly every year. Secondly, a rapidly aging work force is hampered by the one-child policy of past regimes and diminishing factory-age work force. Last but not least, the high cost of training and housing for a short-term transient work force.










What Marketing Strategy?

Three Types of Clients
There are cost, comprehensive service, and specialty types of clients in China. Each company tends to target its own type of clients based on its own capacity, ability and resources in the robotics industry.



What Business Strategy?

Localization
Localization is used because there are many National policies implemented that might significantly affect a foreign enterprise’s business without advanced warning. Also, cultural differences would affect how the first line workers operate a company’s robot. Therefore, I think it is very important to hire a person who has plant-manufacturing experience in China and is able to speak English and Mandarin. If the company lacks an employee with these skills, it should partnership with another local robotics company.
